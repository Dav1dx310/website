Spaghetti alle vongole recipe:

Ingredients:

Olive oil

2.2 lbs manilla clam

3 whole large Thai chilli minced

6 large garlic cloves thinly sliced

1/4 cup white wine

.75 lb pasta

Chopped Parsley

1 ladle of pasta water

Directions:

On a large saute pan, coat a table spoon's worth of olive oil on low heat. Add the garlic, thai chilli pepper and saute until fragrant. (Do not burn) Increase the heat, then add clams and white wine. Make sure to thoroughly mix well with a tong. Cover w/ a lid and cook until the clams open up. When this is done, add the al dente pasta noodles, chopped parsley and a ladle of pasta water. (The startch thickens the pasta sauce) Drizzle with additonal tablespoon of olive, sprinkle cracked black peppercorn (optional) and stir everything well for a minute or two.
